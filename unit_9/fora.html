Client—FORA.tv

Agency—Freelance

Problem

FORA.tv is a company that offers services for event video production, online distribution and monetization. The company currently serves more than 500 organizations, primarily working with event and conference organizers. The website needed an upgrade so that they could reach to a more younger, intelligent audience.

Solution

The design was inspired by theaters, where viewing is immersive and free of distraction. The aim was to deliver a similar experience for viewing online videos. In August 2009, FORA.tv was selected for TIME.com's 50 Best Websites.
