    brand identity design

Client—Hospital Corporatation of America

Agency—Interbrand Health, New York

Problem

Hospital Corporation of America was starting a financing consultancy that needed a new verbal and visual identity. Interbrand Health, a pharma-focused branding division within Interbrand, was asked to present concepts for the new brand.

Solution

Caduceus of mercury, has been used as a symbol for medicine and healing for centuries. Healthcare professionals often used this symbol in their logos, letterheads and signage. This familiar visual cue of the two serpents coiled around a staff was used as a starting point for the design exploration. Then by turning it sideways, it began to represent process, movement and flow. The logo was further simplified to give it a more contemporary and modern expression.
