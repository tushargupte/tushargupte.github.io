    • 6—IBM Cloud
    Site Redesign

Client—IBM

Agency—IBM Studio, New York

Problem

Cloud computing has been one of the key strategic focus areas for IBM since the last few years. However, the user experience of the site—IBM.com/cloud—was not up-to-date. The task was to redesign the site, while ensuring the new design is based on IBM's native content management framework.

Solution

As part of the site redesign, a visual language with modern typography, people-focused photography, and a responsive site was proposed and implemented. This redesign became a benchmark for the new IBM.com digital brand experience going forward.
